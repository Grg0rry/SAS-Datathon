    Overview

    The Carsome Data Innovative Hackathon 2022 is a four-day hackathon whereby students will work in teams of 4 to 5 to build prediction models and deliver insights from a used car database from Carsome.


    This hackathon is brought to you by Sunway Analytics Society in collaboration with Sunway iLabs Foundry and Carsome Malaysia.

    the Benefits

    Practical Experience

    Opportunity to apply technical knowledge in programming and data analytics to a real-world context.

    Mentoring Opportunity

    Opportunity to be mentored by professionals in the industry.

    Cash Prizes

    Stand a chance to win attractive prizes worth up to RM4500!

    Participants Eligibility

  • Attended at least 2 of the workshops from Exploring the Wonders of Data Science.
  • Is a student from Sunway University/College or a Cadets from 42KL.
  • Have strong knowledge in analytics/programming language.
